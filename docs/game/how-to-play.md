# How to Play — World Hopper: The Seven Signals

A quick guide for explorers. Glint the weather-spark has scattered the seven **Signal Lenses** across Sunspire Village — your team recovers them before the last sunset and relights the Wayfinder Beacon.

> **Explorer Rule:** Animals are neighbors, not game pieces. Watch from a respectful distance, never touch or feed wildlife, and follow staff and posted signs. A photo is never required to finish a quest.

---

## 1. Start your trip

1. On the title screen, tap **"Start Our Trip ✨"** (or **Continue** to resume a saved game).
2. **Name your team** (e.g. "The Tidefinders").
3. **Add 2–4 explorers.** Nicknames are fine — no real names needed.
4. **Pick a role** for each explorer. Roles add flavor and hints — **any explorer can do any activity**:
   - **Inventor** — fixes mechanisms and finds shortcuts
   - **Pathfinder** — spots clues and hidden paths
   - **Ocean Guardian** — handles kindness and wildlife moments
   - **Trail Ranger** — plans routes and map puzzles
5. Tap **"Begin the Adventure ✨"** to open the island map.

Your first clue appears: *"The first light rests where water meets wonder."*

---

## 2. The main loop (repeat for each chapter)

1. **Walk your explorer** around the island with the **arrow keys / WASD** or the on-screen **D-pad**.
2. **Reach a glowing landmark**, then tap **▶ Explore** — or just tap a landmark pin directly.
3. **Read the scene** — tap to advance the dialogue.
4. **Choose how to play** (tap **"How to play & real-world version"** to see both):
   - **In the game** — play the chapter's mini-game, or
   - **On your trip** — do the real-world version with a grown-up, or
   - tap **"Mark complete without playing"** to skip ahead.
5. **Recover the Lens!** You earn a **Signal Lens**, a **badge**, and team **stats**.
6. **Add a memory** — write a short reflection in your **Memory Book** (optional).

The objective bar at the top of the map always tells you where to go next.

---

## 3. The seven chapters

Collect one Lens per chapter. Start with the first three in any order; the rest unlock as you go.

| # | Chapter | Place | Lens | Mini-game |
|---|---------|-------|------|-----------|
| 1 | The Quiet Shell | Turtleglass Lagoon | Tide (teal) | Catch falling shells in your basket |
| 2 | The Station That Moved | Sunline Tram | Route (gold) | Switch tracks to collect tickets |
| 3 | Echoes in the Hall | Hall of Echoes | Echo (violet) | Tap the glowing echo orbs |
| 4 | The Bridge of Brave Steps | Splashbridge Basin | Spark (coral) | Hop the platforms, grab sparks, reach the 🚩 |
| 5 | The Fourfold Flow | Fourfold Springs | Flow (blue) | Catch the falling water drops |
| 6 | The Lantern Listens | Lantern Evening | Lantern (amber) | Tap the lantern lights |
| 7 | The Sunset Nobody Keeps | Palmwind Paths | Sunset (rose) | Find your way through the garden maze |

**Unlocking:** Lagoon, Tram, and Hall are open from the start. Splashbridge opens after you finish **1** early chapter; Springs and Palmwind after **2**. Lantern Evening opens once you hold **5 lenses**.

---

## 4. Win the game

1. **Collect all 7 lenses.**
2. The objective bar says: *"All seven lenses recovered! Return to the Beacon Courtyard."*
3. **Tap the Beacon Courtyard** (the lighthouse at the island's center).
4. **Place the lenses in order**, naming each lesson:
   **Care → Curiosity → Memory → Courage → Teamwork → Listening → Creativity**
5. The Wayfinder relights and your team earns the title **"Keepers of the Summer Signals."** 🎉

After winning you can revisit your Memory Book and explore bonus **Outer-Island Expeditions**.

---

## 5. Your screens (top of the map)

- 🎒 **Field Kit** — your team stats (Discovery, Kindness, Creativity, Ingenuity), the 7 lenses, and badges earned.
- 📖 **Memory Book** — every reflection you've written, newest first.
- ⚙️ **Settings** — toggle **Sound**, **Reduced motion**, and **Easy-reading font**; **export/import** your save; or **reset** the adventure.

---

## 6. Good to know

- **Gentle by default — no way to lose.** No timers or lives; falling just respawns you. Turn on **⚔️ Challenge Mode** in Settings for an arcade version of the Bridge chapter: 3 levels, 3 lives, coins, enemies — where you *can* lose and try again.
- **Nothing is required.** Every chapter can be finished in the game, on your trip, or skipped — closed pools or tired explorers never block you.
- **It saves itself.** Progress is stored automatically after each step and works offline.
- **Play your way.** Turn on reduced motion or the easy-reading font any time in Settings.

Explore kindly — and have a wonderful summer. 🌅

---

## 7. Challenge Mode (optional)

Flip **⚔️ Challenge Mode** on in Settings, then play **The Bridge of Brave Steps**:
pick a hero (🧭 Explorer / 🏴‍☠️ Pirate / 🧟 Zombie) and clear **3 levels** with
**3 lives**. Grab 🪙 coins, jump on enemies to squash them (a touch from the side
costs a life), and reach the 🚩 flag. Lose all your lives and it's **Game Over** —
tap **Try Again** to restart from Level 1. The rest of the game is unchanged.
